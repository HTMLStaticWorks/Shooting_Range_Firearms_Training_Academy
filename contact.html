DIRECTIONS & PARKING

To keep our environment safe and secure, please review our gate entry and parking guidelines before arriving at the academy.

Gate Entry

Show your membership card or class enrollment confirmation barcode at the main security kiosk for entry.

Ample Parking

Dedicated parking slots are available for members and first-time guests right in front of the main lobby.

Firearm Transport

All firearms brought onto the premises must be cased and unloaded. No open carry in parking or lobby areas.
