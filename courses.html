PREPARATION

WHAT TO BRING

Proper gear ensures a safe and productive training session. Please review our mandatory checklist before arriving.

  • Valid ID: Government-issued photo ID is required for all classes.
  • Eye & Ear Protection: Electronic hearing protection is highly recommended for situational awareness.
  • Appropriate Attire: Closed-toe shoes and high-neck shirts (to prevent hot brass from causing burns).
