ABOUT ELITE ACADEMY

Forging elite marksmen through discipline, safety, and world-class instruction.

Our Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2010 by former military and law enforcement professionals, Elite Academy was built on a single premise: to provide civilian and professional shooters with the highest caliber of firearms training available.

We believe that owning a firearm comes with a profound responsibility. Our mission is to equip every student with the knowledge, skills, and mindset necessary to handle firearms safely, effectively, and confidently under any circumstance.

  • Advanced HEPA air filtration turning over air every 90 seconds
