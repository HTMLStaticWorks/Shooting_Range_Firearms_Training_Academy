SAFETY FIRST

RANGE RULES

Strict adherence to these rules ensures a safe environment for everyone.

  1. Always keep the firearm pointed in a safe direction.
    Downrange at all times. Never sweep the muzzle across anything you are not willing to destroy.
  2. Treat every firearm as if it is loaded.
    Check the chamber visually and physically upon picking up any firearm.
  3. Keep your finger off the trigger until ready to shoot.
    Rest your index finger on the frame above the trigger guard until your sights are on target.
  4. Know your target and what is beyond it.
    Ensure your bullets impact the backstop directly. No cross-lane shooting allowed.
  5. Obey the Range Safety Officer (RSO) at all times.
    The RSO has absolute authority. If "CEASE FIRE" is called, immediately stop shooting and bench your firearm.
