Earn your badges and track your progress through our elite curriculum.

The Elite Certification Path

Our curriculum is designed to take you from foundational safety to advanced tactical proficiency. Each level unlocks new training opportunities and exclusive range privileges.

  • Nationally recognized curriculum
  • Expert instructor evaluations
  • Progressive skill-building

Certification Tiers

Level I: Safety

Mastery of essential firearm safety rules, basic handling, and stance.

Prerequisite: None

Level II: Marksman

Consistent accuracy, drawing from a holster, and malfunction clearing.

Prerequisite: Level I

Level III: Tactical

Movement and shooting, multiple target engagement, and barricade use.

Prerequisite: Level II
ELITE

Level IV: Master

Low-light tactics, advanced defensive scenarios, and competition-level speed.

Prerequisite: Level III
EVALUATION METRIC

HOW YOU ARE EVALUATED

Each tier has objective safety and accuracy metrics. We do not compromise on security or skill standards.

01
Prep & Class

Complete the respective class level and log the recommended practice hours with our certified trainers.

02
Safety Audit

Pass a flawless range safety inspection checking muzzle direction control, holster draws, and reloads.

03
Shooting Qual.

Complete the timed qualification course with a minimum accuracy score (e.g. 90% in center rings).

04
Badge Ceremony

Receive your official certificate, custom physical badge, and upgraded privileges on your ID card.
